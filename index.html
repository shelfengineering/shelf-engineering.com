About

The system that decides what gets discovered is being rewritten. Not by committees or regulations — by language models processing billions of queries, making billions of choices about what to surface and what to ignore.

Shelf Engineering exists to understand that shift and prepare for it. We publish the research, build the tools, and explain the mechanics — honestly, with sources, and without the noise that dominates this space.
