LLM adoption.

User growth across six AI platforms. ChatGPT reports weekly active users; the rest report monthly. Meta AI counts include users across WhatsApp, Instagram, and Messenger.

ChatGPT numbers are weekly active users (WAU). All others are monthly active users (MAU). Meta AI users are embedded in existing Meta apps, not a standalone product. DeepSeek numbers are third-party estimates.
